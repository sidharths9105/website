About the FOSS Club

The FOSS Club at LBSCEK is a student-run organization that aims to:

  • Promote the use of Free and Open Source Software.
  • Organize workshops, hackathons, and coding events.
  • Foster a community of learners and contributors.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge and skills to contribute to the open-source ecosystem.
